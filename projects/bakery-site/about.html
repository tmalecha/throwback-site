About Us

Located in the heart of Kremmling, Colorado, Big Shooter Coffee is the perfect place to stop on your way to Steamboat, back to Denver, or if you need a treat!

Owners Stephanie and Shawn Scoll have been serving those who live and travel through Grand County for over 20 years and it's their mission to provide a top quality product and service.

We recycle our coffee grounds. We purchase eco-friendly paper products. We are currently looking into acquiring solar panels to heat our water, and photovoltaic panels for electricity.We shop locally as much as possible, and try to spend money in as many different businesses as available.

Additionally, we work closely with the community and encourage local shopping. We can partner with your business or any community based function and some of our profits will be donated to local schools.

Come in, play some games, drink some coffee, take a picture with our bear, and relax for a while!
