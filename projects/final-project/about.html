About Me



Hi! My name is Teagan Malecha and I am currently a Junior at Belmont University in Nashville, Tennessee. I am currently majoring in Multimedia Productions with a minor in Sports and Media.

I am interning with the Communications Department for the Nashville Predators. Along with that, I work for ESPN+ as a production assistant and sideline reporter for Belmont Athletics.

I have always had a passion for sports, specifically hockey, and I hope to continue making connections and networking to reach my goals.

I am originally from Westminster, Colorado and I moved to Nashville to go to concerts and sports games, and so far I have done both! You can find me in the mountains, at a hockey game, or at a new restaraunt!
